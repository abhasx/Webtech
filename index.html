IILM Branches

  • IILM University Greater Noida

    • The IILM group itself was originally established in 1993, and the Greater Noida campus has been functioning as part of this educational group for over 30 years. The university status was formalized under the Uttar Pradesh Private University (Amendment) Act, 2022

  • IILM University Lucknow

    • It is a management education institute (not a full “university”)

  • IILM University Lodhi Road

    • IILM Lodhi Road is one of the oldest business education campuses of the IILM Group, established in 1993, offering AICTE-approved PGDM and BBA programmes with strong industry exposure and practical learning. It has a heritage of over 30 years in management education with a focused corporate connect

  • IILM University Jaipur

    • IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Jaipur was established in 1998 under the Ram Krishan & Sons Charitable Trust and is a reputed management institute in Rajasthan offering two-year full-time PGDM programmes approved by AICTE. The campus emphasizes experiential learning, global curriculum influences, and industry engagement.

  • IILM University Gurugram

    • IILM University, Gurugram is a private university (established as such in 2018) offering a wide range of UG, PG, and doctoral programmes across management, law, technology, liberal arts, and more, with modern infrastructure and industry-linked education. It focuses on holistic development, research, and global collaborations.
